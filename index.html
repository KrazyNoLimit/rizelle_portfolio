My Story

About Me

I'm a College Graduate in Bachelor of Science Tourism Management with a genuine passion for graphic design and visual editing. It all started as a hobby during my student years—creating logos, page layouts, and custom graphics for friends and school projects—and grew into the creative craft I focus on today.

I believe that effective design doesn't need to be loud or overly complicated. Clean layouts, deliberate spatial choices, and consistent adjustments make a visual experience meaningful and memorable. I'm always eager to learn new tools and refine my craft to bring ideas to life.
